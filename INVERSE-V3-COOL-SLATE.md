# INVERSE VERSION 3: COOL SLATE (Inverse of Warm Coffee Shop)

## Color Scheme
- **Background:** #F1F5F9 (Cool Gray/Blue-White)
- **Text:** #0F172A (Deep Slate)
- **Accent:** #0D9488 (Teal/Ocean)
- **Secondary:** #E2E8F0 (Light Slate)
- **Highlight:** #CCFBF1 (Pale Teal)

## Vibe
Fresh, modern, calming. Like a clean spa, modern co-working space, or wellness center. Professional but relaxed.

## Design Elements
- Cool gray backgrounds (not stark white)
- Teal/turquoise accents
- Clean geometric shapes
- Rounded corners (softer than sharp, but not bubbly)
- Subtle gradients in teal tones
- Minimal shadows, flat design

## Recommended For
Wellness businesses, professional services, consultants, creative agencies—anyone wanting "modern and trustworthy" without being corporate.

```css
--bg-primary: #F1F5F9;

--bg-accent: #CCFBF1;
--text-primary: #0F172A;
--text-secondary: #475569;
--accent: #0D9488;
--accent-light: #5EEAD4;
--border: #CBD5E1;
```

## Typography
- **Headlines:** DM Sans or Plus Jakarta Sans (modern geometric)
- **Body:** Inter or DM Sans
- **Accents:** Medium weight, clean lines

## Button Styles
- Background: #0D9488

- Border: none
